Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy governs the processing of personal data by La Cabra Sonora S.L., operating under the brand DigiTalks AI, in the context of providing services related to audio, localization, and audiovisual production.

1. Purpose and Scope

This policy applies to all activities in which the Company collects and processes personal data.

2. Personal Data Collected

  • Identification data: full name, ID number, postal address, phone number, email address.
  • Professional data: job title, company, country of residence.
  • Contractual and tax data: Social Security number, billing and payment information.
  • Biometric and voice data: recordings, audio samples.
  • Technical information: IP address, metadata derived from the use of digital platforms.

These data may be collected through forms, emails, contracts, recordings, and digital tools used by the Company.

3. Purposes of Data Processing

  • To establish and manage contractual relationships.
  • To carry out audio recording, editing, and voice-over processes.
  • To provide voice generation services using artificial intelligence.
  • To develop professional audiovisual content.
  • To comply with legal, tax, and labor obligations.
  • To manage projects, technical support, and communications.
  • To train AI models, subject to the data subject’s explicit consent.
  • To send communications related to contracted services and projects.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of a contract.
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Explicit consent from the data subject (particularly for voice processing for AI purposes).
  • The Company’s legitimate interest in managing and improving its services.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

Personal data may be disclosed, only when necessary, to:

  • AI platforms that are duly authorized and with informed consent.
  • Clients requesting recordings or auditions, under confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal, tax, or technical advisors working with the Company.
  • Public authorities, in accordance with applicable law.

The Company does not sell, transfer, or share personal data without a legal basis or prior consent.

6. International Transfers

If any service provider hosts data outside the European Economic Area, an adequate level of protection will be ensured through standard contractual clauses or other mechanisms recognized by the GDPR.

7. Data Retention Period

Data will be retained for the time necessary to fulfill the purposes described and as required by applicable regulations. For data related to AI model training, the retention period will be as stipulated in the signed contract or agreement.

8. Data Subject Rights

  • Access to their data.
  • Rectification of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of data where legally applicable.
  • Restriction of processing.
  • Objection to processing.
  • Data portability.
  • Withdrawal of previously given consent.

Requests should be sent to: wearedigitalksai@gmail.com with the subject “Data Protection” and a copy of an identification document.

9. Security Measures

The Company has adopted appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security, integrity, and confidentiality of personal data, preventing unauthorized access or improper use.

10. Policy Changes

The Company reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy. Any changes will be duly communicated through publication on the website or via enabled communication channels.
